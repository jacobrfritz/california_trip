## 🌲 Day 1: Wednesday, June 17 — Redwoods and the Coast
*To maximize your time, head straight south from the airport into the mountains.*

*   **Morning/Early Afternoon: Henry Cowell Redwoods**
    *   **Activity:** Walk the spectacular Redwood Grove Loop Trail among ancient old-growth trees.
    *   **Drive:** SFO ➔ Henry Cowell (~1h 15m)
*   **Late Afternoon/Evening: Santa Cruz**
    *   **Activity:** Check in, walk the Beach Boardwalk for sunset, and dinner on the pier.
    *   **Drive:** Henry Cowell ➔ Santa Cruz (~20m)

---

## 🌉 Day 2: Thursday, June 18 — Big Sur Highlights
*This is your primary driving day, covering the most scenic stretch of the California coast.*

*   **Morning — Pfeiffer Big Sur:** Stop at Pfeiffer Big Sur State Park.
    *   *Drive Time (Santa Cruz to Pfeiffer Big Sur): ~1.5 to 2 hours (via Highway 1).*
*   **Afternoon — Coastal Views:** Cross the Bixby Bridge and stop at the McWay Falls overlook.
    *   *Drive Time (Pfeiffer Big Sur to McWay Falls): ~20 minutes.*
*   **Evening — San Luis Obispo:** Continue south to stay in San Luis Obispo or Pismo Beach.
    *   *Drive Time (McWay Falls to San Luis Obispo): ~2.5 to 3 hours.*

---

## 🌴 Day 3: Friday, June 19 — The Gateway to Southern California

*   **Morning — Santa Barbara:** Drive through the "American Riviera." Stop at Stearns Wharf or the Santa Barbara County Courthouse.
    *   *Drive Time (SLO to Santa Barbara): ~1.5 to 2 hours.*
*   **Afternoon — Malibu:** Wind along the coast through Malibu, stopping at El Matador State Beach.
    *   *Drive Time (Santa Barbara to Malibu): ~1 hour.*
*   **Evening — Final Destination:** Arrive at your chosen beach destination to check in for your final two nights.
    *   *Drive Time (Malibu to Santa Monica/Laguna/Huntington): 45 mins to 2 hours (highly dependent on LA traffic).*

---

## 🏖️ Days 4 & 5: Saturday, June 20 – Sunday, June 21 — Beach Relaxation
*Choose your vibe for the final stretch:*

*   **Option 1: Laguna Beach (Scenic & Artistic)**
    Stunning coves like Main Beach and Crystal Cove. Upscale art galleries and cliffs.
*   **Option 2: Huntington Beach (Classic Surf Culture)**
    "Surf City USA." Huge, flat beaches and the iconic Huntington Beach Pier.
